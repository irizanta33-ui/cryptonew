What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency that uses blockchain technology for secure and transparent transactions.

  • Blockchain: A distributed ledger that records all transactions.
  • Decentralization: No central authority; the network validates transactions.
  • Wallets & Keys: Private keys sign transactions, while public addresses receive funds.
